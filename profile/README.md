## Project Overview

![](https://github.com/AutoDRIVE-Ecosystem/.github/blob/main/images/AutoDRIVE-Overview.png)

<p align="justify">
AutoDRIVE is envisioned to be an open, comprehensive, flexible and integrated ecosystem for enhancing autonomous driving research and education. It bridges the gap between software simulation and hardware deployment by providing AutoDRIVE Simulator and AutoDRIVE Testbed, a well-suited duo for real2sim2real applications. It also offers AutoDRIVE Devkit, a developer's kit for flexible development and seamless deployment of autonomy algorithms. The ecosystem supports indoor/outdoor, on-road/off-road autonomous driving across varying scales and configurations.
</p>
